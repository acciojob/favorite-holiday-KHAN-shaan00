Manali, India


Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh known for its snowy mountains, peaceful valleys, adventure sports, and cool weather. It is a perfect holiday destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for paragliding and skiing
  2. Rohtang Pass – Known for snow-covered mountains
  3. Hadimba Temple – A historic wooden temple
  4. Manu Temple – Dedicated to sage Manu
  5. Old Manali – Popular for cafes and shopping
  • Siddu – A famous Himachali steamed bread
  • Thukpa – A hot Tibetan noodle soup
  • Trout Fish – Fresh river fish dish
  • Babru – Himachali style stuffed kachori
  • Mittha – Traditional sweet rice dessert
